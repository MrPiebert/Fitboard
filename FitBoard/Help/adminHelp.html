Welcome to FitBoard Help

FitBoard is designed to help you manage your fitness programs, track your progress, and stay connected with the weights room. This help section will guide you through various features and functionalities of the application.

Getting Started

  • Dashboard:This is where students can view various programs and where admin can view and modify student details.
  • Profile: Update your profile picture, view personal details and record a biography.
  • Modify Programs: Create and edit fitness programs tailored to your goals.
  • Notifications: Stay updated with the latest announcements and messages from your coaches or fitness community.
  • Video Tutorials: Access a library of instructional videos to help you perform exercises correctly.
  • Settings: Customize your FitBoard experience, including display preferences and account settings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create a new fitness program?

A: Navigate to the "Modify Programs" section from the sidebar, and click on "Create New Program". Fill in the required details and save your program.

Q: How can I change my profile picture?

A: Go to the "Profile" section, click on your current profile picture, and upload a new image.

Q: What should I do if I forget my password?

A: Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.
