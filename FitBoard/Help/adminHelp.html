Welcome to FitBoard Admin Help

Your role an Admin enables you to upload videos, modify programs, make notifications. This help section will guide you through various features and functionalities of the application.


Getting Started

      • Dashboard: This is where you manage the student database and oversee all user activities.
        To manually add a student, press the + Add Student button and enter the required details: SBHS ID, First Name, Last Name, Year, and Tier. Additionally, you can search for students using filters, update their records, and remove inactive users. The dashboard also provides real-time performance metrics and reports, allowing you to monitor overall engagement and progress across the platform.
      • Profile: As an admin, you can update your profile picture, view your personal details, and record a biography. You also have the ability to view and edit user profiles, including changing roles and access levels, and ensuring that all information is accurate and up to date. This section allows you to maintain control over user permissions and ensure the integrity of the platform.
      • Modify Programs: Create, edit, and assign fitness programs tailored to specific student groups or individual users. You can use pre-set templates or design custom programs based on specific goals, levels, and requirements. Additionally, you have the ability to monitor the effectiveness of these programs through detailed analytics, allowing you to make adjustments as necessary to optimize results.
      • Notifications: As an admin, you can send out notifications to specific users or groups, ensuring that important updates and announcements are communicated effectively. You can customize notification settings, such as priority levels and delivery methods, and track who has viewed the notifications. This feature helps you keep all users informed and engaged with the platform.
      • Video Tutorials: Access and manage the library of instructional videos. You can upload new tutorials, categorize them by difficulty and focus area, and assign them to specific programs. Additionally, you can track user engagement with these videos, ensuring that users are benefiting from the content provided.
      • Settings: Customize the overall FitBoard experience for all users. You have the ability to adjust global settings, such as display preferences, notification settings, and user permissions. Manage account settings for all users, including password resets and security measures. You also have the ability to set and monitor platform-wide goals and achievements, ensuring that FitBoard is optimized for all users.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create a new fitness program?

A: Navigate to the "Modify Programs" section from the sidebar, and click on "Create New Program". Fill in the required details and save your program.

Q: How can I change my profile picture?

A: Go to the "Profile" section, click on your current profile picture, and upload a new image.

Q: What should I do if I forget my password?

A: Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

Q: How to add a video?

A: Naviage to the Upload_Video tab and press the green upload button. Then follow the prompts pop up by first inputting the youtube url link. Enter a title and then a description.

Q: How to send a notification?

A: Navigate to the Notifications tab to manage notifications and add them. Select the create notification button, input your desired content and click confirm. You also have the ability to add/remove notifications.
