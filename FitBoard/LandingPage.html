About FitBoard

FitBoard is dedicated to improving the fitness and well-being of Sydney Boys High School students. We provide fitness programs and resources to help students achieve their health goals in collaboration with the Weights Room. Our mission is to create a supportive environment where every student can thrive physically and mentally. As we enter the age of technology we find that young boys are much more inclined to be less physically fit than ideal. We aim to encourage biys to work on themselves as well as a fun, interactive website where they can track their progress and have discussions with their peers and teachers. Our program also makes it much easier for teachers and supervisors to monitor boys and see their progress and check in on their wellbeing.
